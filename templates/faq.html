FAQ:

  1. What is this built with?

    Gumroad is built on top of Google's App Engine and uses a lovely combination of HTML/CSS/Javascript for front-end stuff and Python for back-end stuff. There was no Photoshop involved in the process of building Gumroad - except for the favicon! We use Stripe for payments and Crate for hosting files.

  2. What can I charge per link?

    Each link must be at least $0.99 and can go up to $999.00 — you can specify free links too, if you so choose. Free links will redirect automatically and viewers will never hit a payment wall.

  3. What is Gumroad's fee?

    We have a tiered system for pricing:

    • Below $10/month in sales, we take a 30% cut.

    • Then below $50/month in sales, we take a 25% cut.

    • Then below $200/month in sales, we take a 20% cut.

    • Then below $500/month in sales, we take a 15% cut.

    • On anything about $1000/month in sales, we take a 10% cut.

    We also take $0.30 from each transaction to cover merchant fees.

  4. How do I get paid?

    We use PayPal to pay our users. However, we are looking into - and will definitely implement - mailed checks.

  5. What's with the name?

    I had the domain. No, seriously. I think a lot of people get caught up with thinking about a name and never even end up releasing a product. So I decided I would stick wth a domain I already had and build something. Two days later, I had.

  6. Who made Gumroad?

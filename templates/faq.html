FAQ:

  1. What is this built with?

    Gumroad is built on top of Google's App Engine and uses a lovely combination of HTML/CSS/Javascript for front-end stuff and Python for back-end stuff. There was no Photoshop involved in the process of building Gumroad - except for the favicon! We use Stripe for payments and Crate for hosting files.

  2. What can I charge per link?

    Each link must be at least $0.99 and can go up to $999. If you need something else, please send us an email.

  3. What is Gumroad's cut?

    Simple. It's a flat 5%.

    We also take $0.30 from each card transaction to cover Credit Card interchange fees.

  4. How do I get paid?

    We use PayPal to pay our users. You get paid at the end of every month. However, we are looking into - and will definitely implement - other alternatives.

  5. What's with the name?

    I had the domain. No, seriously. I think a lot of people get caught up with thinking about a name and never even end up releasing a product. So I decided I would stick wth a domain I already had and build something. Two days later, I had.

  6. Who made Gumroad?

    I did. My name's Sahil Lavingia and you can read more about me by clicking that link in the footer. Put simply, I feel like shit when I'm not making stuff. Gumroad is just one of the many things I've built. There's much more to come so you should follow me on Twitter if that interests you.
